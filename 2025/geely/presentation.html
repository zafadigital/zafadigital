Our 3-Pillar Content Strategy

Each pillar moves customers from interest to action.

Pillar 1: Drive Showroom Walk-Ins

Content: Test Drive Experiences, Limited-Time Offers, Showroom Events.

Pillar 2: Generate Quality Inboxes

Content: Feature Spotlights, "Ask Me Anything" sessions, Direct Price Inquiries.

Pillar 3: Build Community & Lifestyle

Content: Customer Stories, "Geely in the Wild" photo contests, Interactive Polls.

Pillar 1: Drive Showroom Walk-Ins

Creating urgency and a compelling reason to visit in person.

Test Drive Experience

High-energy videos showcasing the thrill of driving a Geely, with a clear call-to-action.

Limited-Time Offers

Promote exclusive, time-sensitive deals to create a fear of missing out (FOMO).

Showroom Events

Host and promote special events like a new model launch party to attract foot traffic.

Pillar 2: Generate Quality Inboxes

Engaging serious buyers and prompting them to ask for more information.

Feature Spotlights

Detailed carousels and videos that highlight a single, high-value feature.

"Ask Me Anything"

Live video sessions with a sales expert answering questions from the comments.

Direct Price Inquiry

Posts with a clear call to "Inbox us for the best price" to capture high-intent leads.

Pillar 3: Build Community & Lifestyle

Connecting with the audience on an emotional level to reinforce brand identity.

Customer Stories

Feature real Geely owners in Cambodia, sharing their photos and quotes.

"Geely in the Wild"

Encourage users to share photos of their Geely with a hashtag for a chance to win.

Interactive Polls

Engage the audience with fun polls like 'Which color is your favorite?' to boost interaction.
